# Connect to Outside of form

Formalite can connect and and validate your custom input outside of Formalite object

we can place custom component two place:
* Outside of Formalite
* Inside of Formalite

### Outside of Formalite

we can add Rating view with not included in Formalite added after Formalite form and when form submitted this rating and get validated and submitted with Formalite form
